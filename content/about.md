I'm Josh, an aerospace engineering graduate student at Georgia Tech
(BS '24, MS expected '27) focused on propulsion. This site is my reading
log for the rotating detonation engine literature: paper reviews,
derivations, and small original analyses as I work through the field.

Outside of coursework I've built and machined hardware with Georgia Tech
Experimental Rocketry (GTRX) and worked in GTRI's Advanced Concepts Lab,
so the posts here tend to care about how things are actually fabricated
and tested, not just simulated.

If you work in pressure-gain combustion or detonation propulsion and want
